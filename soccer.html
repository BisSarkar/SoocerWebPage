  1. The sport of soccer (called football in most of the world) is considered to be the world's most popular sport.
  2. In soccer there are two teams of eleven players. Soccer is played on a large grass field with a goal at each end.
  3. The object of the game is to get the soccer ball into the opposing team's goal.
  4. Perhaps the most famous soccer tournament is the World Cup. Held every four years.
  5. The skill at which great players and great soccer teams work the ball, strategize, and flow as one can be an awesome thing to watch.
